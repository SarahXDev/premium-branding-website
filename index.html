Need Answers?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

What's included in your development process?

Our comprehensive process includes discovery, strategy, design, development, testing, launch, and ongoing support. Each phase includes client collaboration and approval checkpoints.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A standard website takes 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive digital transformations can take 12-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during discovery.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, all projects include 3 months of complimentary support. After that, we offer maintenance plans starting at $99/month that include updates, security patches, and priority support.

What technologies do you work with?

We use modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, and various CMS platforms. We choose the best stack for each project based on requirements and scalability needs.

Can I see examples of your work?

Absolutely. We have a curated portfolio of case studies available. For proprietary projects, we can share examples under NDA during a consultation call.

What makes your service "premium"?

Our premium service includes dedicated project managers, custom solutions (no templates), hand-coded development, AI integration, 24/7 support, and a focus on luxury user experiences.
